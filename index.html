When and where is Ignite?

Ignite-2019 is on 15th and 16th of February and will be held at the UPES Campus.

How do I get to Ignite-2019?

The UPES campus is well connected by public transport and is surrounded by amazing scenic beauty. You can easily navigate through Google Maps and in case you are lost, feel free to contact us.

How can I participate in Ignite-2019?

The registrations are open for everyone and you can register at the IGNITE registration desk , WiFi zone, UPES .

Where to go if I have questions regarding the event?

Ignite has its volunteers all around the campus and at all the events. You can approach them at any time and with any query.
